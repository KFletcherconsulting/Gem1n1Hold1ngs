A Third Vertical

Capital and partnership for small business.

Alongside our real estate and land development portfolio, Gem1n1 Hold1ngs invests directly in established, cash-flowing small businesses — bringing capital, operating discipline, and a long-term partnership mindset to owners who are ready to grow, recapitalize, or step back.

Why We Invest in Small Business

The same underwriting discipline, a different asset class.

The approach that guides our real estate and land acquisitions — buy right, professionalize operations, build durable cash flow — applies just as well to small business. We look for owners who've built something real and want a partner for the next chapter, not a buyer looking to strip and flip the business.

Who We Look For

Owners ready for a true partner.

Whether you're looking to sell a majority stake, bring in growth capital, or explore succession planning, we structure around what the business and its owner actually need — not a one-size-fits-all term sheet.

Partnership Offerings

Four ways we structure a deal.

Every business and every owner's goals are different — these are the core structures we work from, often blended to fit the specific situation.

01

Equity Partnership

We acquire a meaningful equity stake in exchange for capital and hands-on operational support, with the existing owner remaining involved at a level that fits their goals.

02

Revenue-Share Financing

Non-dilutive capital provided in exchange for a fixed percentage of top-line revenue until an agreed return is reached — ownership stays exactly where it is.

03

Growth Capital

Minority investment earmarked for a specific expansion, equipment purchase, or working-capital need, sized to the opportunity rather than the whole business.

04

Acquisition Partnership

For full ownership transitions, we can partner directly with an operator to acquire the business together, pairing our capital with day-to-day operating leadership.

Investment Criteria

What we look for in a business.

Track Record

Established cash flow

Typically several years of operating history and consistent profitability, not an early-stage startup.

Footprint

Nationwide footprint

Businesses operating anywhere in the country, evaluated on fundamentals rather than geography.

Essential Services

Durable, needs-based demand

Home services, healthcare-adjacent, and other essential-service categories we understand deeply from our real estate operations.

Owner Fit

An owner open to partnership

Whether staying on or transitioning out, an owner who wants a genuine partner rather than a purely transactional exit.

Deal Size

Right-sized for a close relationship

We intentionally invest at a scale where our team stays closely involved, not spread across dozens of portfolio companies.

Clean Structure

Straightforward ownership & books

Clear financials and a simple ownership structure that allow us to move through diligence efficiently.

How It Works

From first conversation to close.

Step 1

Introductory conversation

A confidential, no-obligation conversation to understand your business and what you're hoping to accomplish.

Step 2

Initial review

We review high-level financials and operations to confirm mutual fit before either side invests significant time.

Step 3

Underwriting & diligence

A deeper look at financials, operations, and growth potential, run on a timeline that respects your business's day-to-day demands.

Step 4

Term sheet

We put forward a clear, straightforward structure — equity, revenue-share, growth capital, or a blend — with no hidden terms.

Step 5

Partnership close

Once terms are agreed, we move to close efficiently, then begin working alongside you as a genuine operating partner.
