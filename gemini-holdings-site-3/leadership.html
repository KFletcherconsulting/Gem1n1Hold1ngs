How We're Organized

Our Team & Operations

Gem1n1 Hold1ngs LLC is a privately held, anonymous limited liability company. In keeping with the structure our ownership has chosen, we describe how the firm operates below rather than publishing individual names or biographies.

Ownership Structure

An anonymous LLC, by design.

Gem1n1 Hold1ngs operates as an anonymous limited liability company. This structure is common in private real estate, land, and small business investment, and allows the firm's principals to conduct business, negotiate, and hold assets nationwide without their personal identities attached to public filings.

Why It Matters to Partners

Privacy for principals, clarity for you.

Anonymity protects our principals' personal privacy and security; it does not affect the legitimacy of the entity, the enforceability of agreements, or our ability to close on capital, real estate, or business transactions. Every partnership is documented through standard, fully binding legal agreements.
